About Me

Ebrahim is a Senior Security Engineer with over 8 years of experience in Information Security that varies from extensive experience in Web Applications Penetration Testing with a great worldwide achievements, and good experience in Network Security, Systems Engineering, Threat Intelligence, Malware behavior analysis, DNS and other Logs Analysis, Cryptography and Developing Security related applications. Also he has been a speaker at multiple international conferences & Instructor for different Penetration testing courses.

Acknowledged and rewarded by Google, Microsoft, Twitter, Yahoo, Facebook, Kaspersky, Avira, Yandex, Paypal, Ebay, AT&T, Barracuda and Telekom.com & More. Also Ranked as #1 Security researcher for Yahoo Several times for finding over 50 Vulnerability in Yahoo!.
